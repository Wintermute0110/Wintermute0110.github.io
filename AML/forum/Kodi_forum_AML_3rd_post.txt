[size=large][b][color=#6b8e23]Bugs & Feature Requests[/color][/b][/size]

You can ask for feature requests in this thread or directly on [url=https://github.com/Wintermute0110/plugin.program.AML]GitHub[/url].

If you find a bug or Kodi shows an error in AEL you can report it in this thread or in [url=https://github.com/Wintermute0110/plugin.program.advanced.MAME.launcher]GitHub[/url]. Try to be descriptive about the problem and the steps you do that cause it. Unless otherwise told, follow these steps:

[list]
[*]In Kodi Settings, [b]turn Kodi debug log OFF[/b].
[*]In AEL Settings, [b]go to Advanced and set the log level to DEBUG[/b].
[*]Reproduce the bug.
[*]Put your [url=http://kodi.wiki/view/Log_file/Advanced]Kodi log file[/url] in [url=http://paste.ubuntu.com/]Ubuntu pastebin[/url] or [url=http://pastebin.com/]PasteBin[/url].
[/list]

You can use the [url=http://forum.kodi.tv/showthread.php?tid=288153]Kodi Logfile Uploader[/url] addon to help with the log upload process. Thanks to Ronie for this fine addon.
